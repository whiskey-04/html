About

[Record]. A Warm Vintage Space Where Music Lives OnStep into our cozy little shop, where the soft crackle of vinyl fills the air, and the scent of old wood brings you back in time. Record is more than just a record store. it's a space created by music lovers, for music lovers. Our shelves are lined with carefully selected vinyl records from all eras and genres: from jazz, soul, classic rock, pop, and folk to indie gems and timeless Thai melodies. The atmosphere is warm and inviting, with vintage furniture, soft lighting, and classic posters on the walls. Whether you're a seasoned collector or simply looking for something new to listen to, you're always welcome here. Come to browse, to talk, to discover new sounds or just to sit back and let the music take you somewhere you’ve missed.
